About Our Church

A community of faith, hope, and love serving our neighborhood and beyond.

Our Story

A Journey of Faith

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and spiritual guidance in our community for over 35 years.

Our Humble Beginnings

Grace Community Church began as a small gathering of families who shared a vision for a welcoming, inclusive place of worship. What started in a living room with just 12 members has grown into a vibrant community of over 500 faithful individuals.

Through the years, we've remained committed to our founding principles of faith, service, and community. Our growth has allowed us to expand our ministries and outreach programs, touching more lives than we ever thought possible.

Our Mission

Faith, Hope, and Love in Action

Spiritual Growth

We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth through biblical teaching, prayer, and worship that connects people with God's transformative love.

Community Outreach

We believe in extending God's love beyond our walls through service, charity, and outreach programs that address the needs of our local and global communities.

Inclusive Fellowship

We foster a welcoming environment where people of all backgrounds can find belonging, build meaningful relationships, and grow together in faith.

Our Values
